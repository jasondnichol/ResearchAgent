📊 System Overview

The system runs two independent bots on AWS EC2, both paper trading on Coinbase. All long trading routes through Coinbase CFM perpetual futures (0.06% taker fee) as the primary venue, with spot (0.50% fee) as fallback. This all-futures approach is the single biggest return lever — 8x lower fees roughly double net returns.

🔄 The Evolution

Phase 1: Hourly Regime-Based Trading

Three regimes (Ranging, Trending, Volatile) with different strategies each. Looked great in backtest (+57%) — failed in practice (-99%) because Coinbase fees (~0.60% round-trip) were 12x larger than the average hourly gain of ~0.05%.

Phase 2: Daily Donchian Breakout

Pivoted to daily timeframe. Average winning trade: +25.8% gain over 21-day hold. The 0.90% round-trip fee is negligible. Wait for confirmed breakouts on strong volume and ride trends.

Phase 3: Optimization (Feb–Mar 2026)

Added pyramiding (+15% trigger), 4x ATR trailing stops, partial profit-taking, futures execution, short-side trading. Optimized risk to 4% per trade. Combined result: +385% over 4 years.

✅ Entry Rules

Long Entry (Futures Long + Spot Long)

ALL conditions must be true simultaneously:

  • ✓
    Bull filter: BTC daily close > 200-day SMA
  • ✓
    Breakout: Close > previous 20-day Donchian high
  • ✓
    Volume: Volume > 1.5x 20-day average
  • ✓
    Trend: Price > 21-day EMA

Short Entry (Futures Short)

ALL conditions must be true simultaneously:

  • ✓
    Bear filter (death cross): BTC SMA(50) < SMA(200) AND BTC close < SMA(200)
  • ✓
    Breakdown: Close < previous 10-day Donchian low
  • ✓
    Volume: Volume > 2.0x 20-day average (higher threshold)
  • ✓
    Trend: Price < 21-day EMA

🚪 Exit Rules

Long Exits (any one triggers)

📉 Trailing Stop
price < high_watermark - 4 × ATR(14) Primary exit. Locks in gains as price rises, exits on pullback.
📈 Donchian Exit
close < 10-day Donchian low Catches trend reversals via channel breakdown.
🚨 Emergency Stop
price < entry - 15% Hard floor for catastrophic moves. Always active.
💥 Blow-Off Detection
vol > 3x avg AND RSI > 80 → stop = 1.5x ATR Tightens stop during parabolic tops to capture gains.

Short Exits (any one triggers)

📈 Trailing Stop
price > low_watermark + 2 × ATR(14) Inverted trailing stop. Follows price down, exits on bounce.
📉 Donchian Exit
close > 15-day Donchian high Catches trend reversals via channel breakout.
🚨 Emergency Stop
price > entry + 15% Hard floor for runaway rallies.
⏳ Max Hold
hold_days > 30 Prevents stale short positions from lingering.
